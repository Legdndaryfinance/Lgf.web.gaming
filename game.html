The Legendary Game Experience

Immerse yourself in our play-to-earn ecosystem where skill meets reward. LGF tokens are the lifeblood of our gaming universe, enabling true digital ownership and real-world value.

Web3-Gaming

1. Core Concept

  • The LGF Token will serve as the primary currency within the Web3 game.
  • The game is designed to deliver an enhanced Web3 experience.
  • It features simple, engaging, and fun Web3-based number games.

2. Revenue Model

  • No entry fees are required for players to participate.
  • Players can only play and claim rewards using the LGF Token.
  • The game is designed for easy wallet integration, allowing players to connect with multiple wallets seamlessly.
  • It is built to ensure fair participation for everyone.
  • VIP Rewards: Players who hold NFTs will gain VIP membership status.
  • Frequent players will be eligible for additional reward bonuses.
  • Token holders during specific periods will have the chance to win valuable prizes.
  • Mini-games or guessing competitions will distribute small LGF token rewards, ensuring engagement even for non-winners.
  • The game will operate without strict win/loss restrictions, providing a free and open gaming environment.
  • Transparent Draws: Game results will be generated using blockchain-based Verifiable Random Functions (VRF) to ensure fairness and transparency.
  • A fraud-free gaming experience for users.
  • Winner Promotions: Winning players will be encouraged to share screenshots on social media to build trust and excitement.
  • Referral Program: Users can earn LGF rewards by inviting others to join the game.
  • Drive LGF token demand through player engagement and participation.
  • In the future, expansion will include additional Web3 mini-games utilizing the same LGF economy.

Summary

  • A fun, fair, and LGF-driven gaming ecosystem.
